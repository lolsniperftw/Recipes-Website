Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Cut the Potatoes into small strips
  2. Put the strips inside the frier
  3. Set the frier to 200 Celsius for 25 minutes
  4. After 12.5 minutes, mix the fries in the bowl and put the bowl back in for the remaining time
  5. After the frier is done, put the fries in a plate and serve with ketchup
